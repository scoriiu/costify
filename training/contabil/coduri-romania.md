### 7. Codul Insolventei -- Legea 85/2014
**Governs insolvency, restructuring, and bankruptcy:**

| Stage | Description | Key Points |
|---|---|---|
| Concordat preventiv | Pre-insolvency agreement | Can restructure debts with creditor approval |
| Observatie | Observation period | Company continues under judicial supervision |
| Reorganizare | Restructuring | Maximum 3-year plan |
| Faliment | Bankruptcy/Liquidation | Asset distribution per priority |
| Procedura simplificata | Simplified procedure | For entities without reorganization prospects |

**Creditor priority in liquidation:**
1. Secured creditors (from secured assets)
2. Procedure costs
3. Salary claims (last 6 months, max 6 average salaries)
4. Budget claims (taxes)
5. Unsecured creditors
6. Subordinated claims
7. Shareholders

## II. LEGI CONTABILE SPECIFICE (Specific Accounting Laws)

### Legea 82/1991 -- Legea Contabilitatii
The foundational accounting law. All entities in Romania must:
- Keep double-entry accounting (partida dubla)
- Use Romanian language and RON currency in books
- Prepare annual financial statements
- File statements with ANAF and at Registrul Comertului
- Retain documents for 10 years minimum

### OMFP 1802/2014 -- Reglementari Contabile
Main accounting regulation. See `legislatie-fiscala.md` for details.

### OMFP 2634/2015 -- Documente Financiar-Contabile
Defines standard forms for all accounting documents.

### OMFP 2844/2016 -- Reglementari contabile IFRS
For entities required to apply IFRS (listed companies, financial institutions).

### OMFP 1826/2003 -- Registrele contabile
Requirements for maintaining accounting registers.

### OMFP 166/2017 -- Situatiile financiare anuale
Annual financial statements format and filing rules.

### OMFP 3781/2019 -- SAF-T Romania (D406)
Standard Audit File for Tax -- electronic reporting of accounting data.

## III. LEGI FISCALE CONEXE (Related Tax Laws)

### Legea 241/2005 -- Evaziunea Fiscala
Defines tax evasion crimes and penalties:
- Art. 3: Omitting to record transactions -- 1-6 years
- Art. 4: Reporting fictitious expenses -- 2-8 years
- Art. 5: Hiding taxable income -- 2-8 years
- Art. 6: Destroying accounting records -- 2-7 years
- Art. 9: Amounts over 100,000 EUR -- aggravated form

### Legea 129/2019 -- Prevenirea si Combaterea Spalarii Banilor (AML)
Anti-money laundering obligations:
- KYC (Know Your Customer) requirements
- Reporting suspicious transactions to ONPCSB
- Reporting cash transactions over 10,000 EUR
- Record keeping for 5 years after relationship ends

### OUG 28/1999 -- Utilizarea aparatelor de marcat electronice fiscale
Cash register obligations:
- All retail sales must use fiscal cash registers
- Electronic journal maintained
- Daily Z report mandatory
- Connected to ANAF system

### Legea 70/2015 -- Intarirea disciplinei financiare
Payment discipline:
- Maximum 60-day payment terms (B2B)
- Maximum 30-day payment terms (B2G)
- Penalties for late payments between businesses

### Legea 53/2003 -- Codul Muncii
See Section I.4 above.

### Legea 227/2015, Titlul V -- Contributii Sociale
Mandatory social contributions (see Codul Fiscal section).

### HG 846/2017 -- Salariul Minim Brut pe Tara
Minimum wage (updated annually/periodically):
- **2026 (Jan-Jun)**: 4,050 RON brut/luna (general)
- **2026 (Jul-Dec)**: 4,325 RON brut/luna (general)
- For social contribution caps, the lower value (4,050) is used for computation
- Updated by government decision -- check annually

### Legea 263/2010 -- Pensii
Pension system:
- Standard retirement age: 65 (men), gradually increasing to 63 (women)
- Contribution period: minimum 15 years
- CAS contribution (25%) funds the public pension system

### OUG 111/2010 -- Concediu Crestere Copil
Parental leave:
- 85% of average net income (last 12 months)
- Maximum 8,500 RON/month
- Duration: until child reaches 2 years (or 3 years for disabled)

### Legea 76/2002 -- Somaj (Unemployment)
Unemployment insurance:
- Duration: 6-12 months depending on contribution period
- Amount: 75% of IAS (Indicatorul Social de Referinta)
- Employer notification obligations

### Legea 153/2017 -- Salarizare Bugetari
Public sector salary framework (relevant for public institution clients).

## VII. LEGI PRIVIND DIGITALIZAREA (Digitalization Laws)

### OUG 120/2021 + OG 13/2024 -- RO e-Factura
Electronic invoicing system:
- **Mandatory B2B** since January 1, 2024
- XML format based on CIUS-RO (EN 16931)
- Invoices uploaded to ANAF SPV within 5 working days
- Non-compliance: 5,000-10,000 RON fine for legal entities

### OUG 1/2022 -- RO e-Transport
Electronic transport monitoring:
- Required for high-risk goods and goods above thresholds
- UIT (Unique Identifier for Transport) code required
- GPS monitoring for certain categories
- Non-compliance: 10,000-50,000 RON fines

### OG 14/2023 -- RO e-TVA
Pre-completed VAT return system:
- ANAF pre-fills D300 based on e-Factura data
- Taxpayer validates/adjusts
- Automatic cross-checking between buyers and sellers

### OMFP 3781/2019 -- SAF-T (D406)
Standard Audit File for Tax:
- XML-based detailed export of all accounting data
- Mandatory for large taxpayers (since 2022)
- Medium taxpayers (since 2023)
- Small taxpayers (phased in 2024-2025)
- Contains: general ledger, customers, suppliers, inventory, fixed assets

### Legea 455/2001 -- Semnatura Electronica
Electronic signature requirements for digital documents.

## XII. CURSURI VALUTARE SI MONEDA (Exchange Rates & Currency)

### BNR Official Exchange Rates
- Published daily by BNR (except weekends/holidays)
- Used for: monthly revaluation of foreign currency balances, tax calculations, customs
- Available at: bnr.ro/cursul-de-schimb

### Currency Rules in Romanian Accounting
- **Functional currency**: RON (Leu romanesc)
- **Recording**: All transactions recorded in RON
- **Foreign currency transactions**: Recorded at BNR rate on transaction date
- **Month-end revaluation**: All FX balances revalued at BNR rate on last business day
- **FX gains**: Account 765 (Venituri din diferente de curs valutar)
- **FX losses**: Account 665 (Cheltuieli din diferente de curs valutar)

### Key Currency Codes
| Currency | ISO | Common Usage |
|----------|-----|---|
| Leu romanesc | RON | All domestic transactions |
| Euro | EUR | EU transactions, some contracts |
| Dolar american | USD | International trade |
| Lira sterlina | GBP | UK transactions |
| Franc elvetian | CHF | Some loans |

## XIII. CONVENTII PENTRU EVITAREA DUBLEI IMPUNERI (Double Tax Treaties)

Romania has DTTs with 90+ countries. Key provisions:
- Withholding tax rates on dividends, interest, royalties
- Permanent establishment definitions
- Tie-breaker rules for residency
- Exchange of information clauses

**Standard withholding rates (without treaty):**
| Income Type | Rate |
|---|---|
| Dividends to non-residents | 16% |
| Interest to non-residents | 16% |
| Royalties to non-residents | 16% |
| Services to non-residents | 16% |

**With EU Parent-Subsidiary Directive**: 0% on dividends to EU parent (min 10% holding, min 2 years)
**With EU Interest/Royalty Directive**: 0% on interest/royalties to EU associated companies
