PRI Discovery — Plan of Action

v1.0 · 8 July 2026 Owner: Engagement Lead Client: Roche Customer Care / PRI Basis: PRI Tool business case + Discovery deck

One AI agent, at the review joint: read 100% of Rexis and Salesforce cases, flag suspected PRIs with written reasoning, and route every flag to the 15 specialists who decide. Minimal organisational footprint on the Roche side: read-only data, one team touched. This plan covers the path from today's approval push to steady-state operations in Q1 2028.

Scope structure (per L. Hermans, 9 July): three product portfolios with different procedures. DC (Diabetes Care) has two procedure variants, EMEA and NAM, with process differences between them, but support itself is global: DC cases also arrive from LATAM, APAC and other regions, and the S0 mapping must state which procedure variant governs each region's cases; NPC (Near Patient Care) and RIS are global. Consequence: the rulebook is not one document but three portfolio rulebooks, DC with two regional variants. Everything below (baseline, golden dataset, MVP scoping, acceptance) is structured per portfolio. And the teams differ too: the 15 specialists are Redsparq's team for their portfolio(s); other Roche teams review the remaining portfolios under their own procedures, headcount unknown. Consequence: the MVP runs on the portfolio our team owns (ground truth in-house); portfolios owned by other teams enter in Stage 2 and need their SMEs for rules and golden-dataset validation, a dependency secured through the sponsor, not assumed.
Two clocks run in parallel in July: (a) Stage 0 authorisation under the existing MSA, so work starts in August regardless of the funding round; (b) the H2 funding submission for Stages 1–3, due before 31 July. Neither waits for the other.

S0

Discovery & Value Baseline

Aug – Sep 2026 · 6–8 wks · €75K
Key actions

  • Golden dataset v1 + adjudication log
  • Versioned rulebook v1 (per portfolio; DC with regional variants)
  • Architecture & GxP classification memo
  • Firm quotes + roadmap for Stages 1–3
Gate 0: signed-off scope, value baseline and roadmap. Funding decision for Stage 1 is made on certified numbers, not estimates.
