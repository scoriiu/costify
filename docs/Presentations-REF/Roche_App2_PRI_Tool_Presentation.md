## Slide 2 — The Problem

# Cases are picked by keywords, not by risk.

Today only a fraction of Rexis and SFDC cases gets a second quality check — and the queue most likely to hide wrongly labelled complaints gets the least attention.

### Inquiries: the blind spot
Wrongly labelled complaints hide mostly in the Inquiry queue — the one reviewed least.

### Keywords are blind
New phrasings, typos, other languages, complaints without trigger words — keyword lists cannot see them.

### Errors surface late
Missed complaints and PRIs found in an audit mean findings, remediation and closer regulatory scrutiny.

*Full human coverage would mean multiplying today's review team many times over — unaffordable, and the market does not supply device-safety QC specialists at that scale. The real alternative is a permanent blind spot.*

---

## Slide 3 — The Solution

# AI reads everything. People decide everything.

### Finds hidden complaints & missed PRIs
Reads up to 100% of Inquiries and Complaints in both Rexis and SFDC — including complaints hiding in the Inquiry queue.

### Flags with reasoning, into the existing queue
Every flag carries its reasoning and source reference, delivered to the Salesforce queue the PRI team already uses.

### Shows where errors come from
By case type, product and team — turned into concrete retraining and intake improvement suggestions.

### Fully auditable
Every model version, prompt and decision logged and traceable, as a regulated environment requires.

**One rule is fixed: the AI searches and prepares, a person always decides.** No record is ever changed automatically. Runs inside Roche's Google Cloud (e.g. Vertex AI) — case data never leaves the Roche environment.
